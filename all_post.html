Exploring Things with Great Purpose: Tour Higlights from A 4-Day Industry Visit to Different Facilities & Companies

Introduction

As a student at Western Mindanao State University majoring in Computing Studies, I am fascinated by new technologies and tech trends. Witnessing various inventions and advanced technologies during the tour was a unique experience. Alongside my friends, who are also passionate about learning new concepts and ideas, we embarked on a four-day tour that opened doors to significant opportunities as aspiring developers.



Visiting SBECC, PAG-ASA, FAA and the Vessel Traffic Management System

Alex Jay
June 30, 2024

As we embarked on our journey to Subic, we were warmly greeted by our tour guide, Ms. Maria Suazo, who is also known as Ms. Lenz. She led us to the Subic Bay Exhibition and Convention Center, a place brimming with history and artistic treasures. Ms. Lenz provided us with a detailed tour, showcasing an array of artworks that highlighted the rich cultural heritage of the area. She also shared fascinating stories and historical facts about Subic, giving us a deeper appreciation of its significance. Her insights and knowledge made the visit both educational and enjoyable, setting the tone for an enriching experience throughout our trip.

The visit to the PAG-ASA Weather Station was highly informative as we learned about its primary role in weather forecasting. We gained insights into weather patterns, structures, and the various hazards associated with them. Additionally, we learned about the key components involved in weather monitoring, such as wind signals and water levels. The experience provided us with a comprehensive understanding of how weather data is collected and analyzed, and the importance of accurate weather monitoring in ensuring public safety.

The FAA provided us with valuable insights into the functioning and maintenance of aircraft, as well as their coordination with PAG-ASA for safe flights and precautionary measures. We learned about the crucial role of communication between these agencies in ensuring aviation safety. Additionally, we experienced the Red Bird technology simulator, which allows for the operation of an aircraft without real-world hazards. This technology enabled us to understand the procedures for running both single-engine and multi-engine aircraft, offering a hands-on perspective of piloting and aircraft management.

The Vessel Traffic Management System was incredibly fascinating, as it tracks the movements of ships and other small vessels crossing Subic Bay. The system is equipped with large monitors, metallic buoys, cameras, and a comprehensive radio system, but the most impressive technology is the Automatic Identification System (AIS). All vessels must be equipped with AIS, which, with the aid of signal towers across the bay, allows for seamless communication and precise tracking of each vessel. This technology ensures the safe and efficient management of maritime traffic, providing an essential service to maintain order and safety in the bay.



Visiting QBO Innovation Hub & Packetworx Inc - IoT Technology Hub

Alex Jay
June 30, 2024

Meeting the QBO secretary, Ms. Karla, was an exciting experience for everyone. She enthusiastically presented QBO's mission, which is to support local developers and entrepreneurs. Ms. Karla encouraged us to support local products, highlighting the importance of community and collaboration. She shared valuable insights and principles that are essential for us as aspiring developers. Her presentation left us inspired and motivated, providing us with knowledge and values that will be beneficial in our future careers.

Our experience with Packetworkx was incredibly cool, showcasing the power of LoRaWAN as an innovative connectivity technology. LoRaWAN impressed us with its affordability, low data consumption, and extensive transmission range. Sir King demonstrated his extensive knowledge of IoT products and presented their software technology, which controls these remarkable hardware items. The demonstration highlighted the potential of LoRaWAN in revolutionizing connectivity and IoT applications.



Visiting LRT - Line 2 & The National Museum

Alex Jay
June 30, 2024

As we continued our journey, the visit to the LRT Line 2 was truly amazing. We had the opportunity to ride the trains on the route from Antipolo to Santolan Station, which was a fun and enjoyable experience. Additionally, we were given access to their main control room, where the railway system is monitored, providing us with a behind-the-scenes look at their operations. The train yard was equally impressive, as we witnessed the meticulous maintenance of the trains and the railway system. This visit gave us a deeper appreciation of the complexities involved in managing and maintaining a modern transit system.

The National Museum of the Philippines provided a captivating narrative of how artists created their masterpieces. We had the privilege of learning about Juan Luna's iconic artwork, "The Spoliarium." The museum's exhibition not only showcased the finished masterpiece but also delved into the creative process behind it. We gained insight into Luna's techniques, inspirations, and the historical context that influenced his work. This experience deepened our appreciation for Filipino artistry and history, offering a profound glimpse into the artistic genius of one of the country's most renowned painters.



Visiting Hytec Power Inc. & 98 Labs Inc.

Alex Jay
June 30, 2024

Visiting Hytec Power Inc. was a remarkable experience as we had the opportunity to interact with robots created by students at the institution. We also participated in simulation games for machinery, such as operating bulldozer mechanisms and welding simulations. Hytec Power Inc. employs XR technology, which encompasses both Virtual Reality (VR) and Augmented Reality (AR). This cutting-edge technology provided us with immersive experiences that blended virtual elements with our physical surroundings, offering a hands-on learning environment that was both educational and enjoyable.

Mr. Brian Tan Seng proved to be an inspirational figure as he generously shared his journey to success as a developer. As the CEO of 98 Labs Inc., meeting him was a once-in-a-lifetime opportunity that enriched our understanding of entrepreneurship and innovation. Mr. Tan Seng imparted invaluable insights on ideation and transforming ideas into reality. His wealth of knowledge provided us with a deeper appreciation of the intricacies involved in building a successful company and navigating the world of technology and business.



Visiting Baguio City

Visiting Baguio City was truly a remarkable experience filled with excitement and joy. Despite the chilly weather, exploring the city's stunning tourist spots was both delightful and awe-inspiring.
